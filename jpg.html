Ruth C. Fong

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in the Visual Geometry Group (VGG) at the University of Oxford, where I am advised by Professor Andrea Vedaldi and funded by the Rhodes Trust and Open Philanthropy. Also at Oxford, I completed a Masters in Neuroscience and worked with Professor Rafal Bogacz as well as Dr. Ben Willmore and Dr. Nicol Harper in the Oxford Auditory Neuroscience Group (ANG).

I received a Bachelors in Computer Science at Harvard University and worked with Professors David Cox and Walter Scheirer while there. I also spent lovely summer months at Microsoft, Apple, and D.E. Shaw.
